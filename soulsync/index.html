// what you get

Everything a mood tracker should do, nothing it shouldn't

No paywall on the stats. No upsell on the themes. The whole app is the free version.

Local SQLite, always

Every entry lives in a SQLite database on your device. Photos too. There is no server to leak, no account to breach.

10-point mood scale

A finer scale than the usual five faces, with high and low precision so you can tell a quiet good day from a great one.

Real entries

Backdate entries you forgot to log. Attach activities, notes, and photos. Set one daily reminder if you want a nudge.

Stats that earn their place

Mood trend with an adaptive moving average, day-of-week patterns, month-over-month, and a heatmap of your whole history.

Activity correlation

A rigorous with-versus-without comparison shows which activities actually move your mood, not just which ones you do a lot.

Plain-language Insights

An Insights tab reads your data and tells you what it sees in normal sentences, not a wall of charts you have to decode.

Five themes

Dark, Light, Cherry Blossom, Midnight Blue, and Forest. Pick the one that fits. None of them cost extra.

You own the export

JSON import and export built in. Your history is a file you control. Move it, back it up, or delete it whenever.

Open source

React Native, Expo, TypeScript, GPL-3.0. The code is public. You don't have to trust a privacy promise, you can read it.
